FAQ

Short notes for the browser build.

What are the different modes?

Maximising Resources — focuses on generating the highest overall resource value, using your Normal and Boosted resource settings to decide which materials should be prioritised.

Fragments — focuses entirely on maximising fragment gain, making it the best choice when fragment farming is your main goal.

Fragments + Resources — prioritises fragment gain first, then uses resource value as a secondary factor to choose the best mission distribution.

Academy Points — focuses on maximising Academy Point income.

Max Completions — focuses on achieving the highest possible number of mission completions.
